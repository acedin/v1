Refund Policy

Please be notified that the refund process takes no less then 14 days from the request date. A refund will be issued according to the original method of payment (e.g. the money will be returned to the cc charged) In case of inability to refund the money to the cc initially charged, you will receive a bank transfer.

A customer can appeal for a refund in the following cases:
The merchandise is not received
The received item does not correspond with the ordered one
The received item is damaged/crushed/torn
The merchandise was lost during delivery/shipping
